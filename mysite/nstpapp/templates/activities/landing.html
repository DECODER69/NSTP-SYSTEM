NSTP COMPONENTS

ROTC

Reserve Officers Training Corps (ROTC) is a program created to give military training to college students in order to inspire, prepare, organize, and mobilize them for national defense readiness.

CWTS

Civic Welfare Training Service (CWTS) is a term used to describe activities that contribute to the general welfare of the community, the betterment of community life, or the enhancement of community facilities, particularly those aimed at enhancing the public's health, education, environment, entrepreneurship, safety, recreation, and morale, as well as other social welfare services.
